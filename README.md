# HostelMate AI – Your Intelligent Student Housing Agent

## Problem Statement

Students relocating to new cities for higher education often face a fragmented and time-consuming accommodation search process. They must browse multiple platforms, compare listings, contact property owners, and verify amenities manually. Most existing rental platforms are designed for general users and fail to address student-specific needs such as affordability, proximity to educational institutions, room-sharing preferences, and hostel facilities.

## Our Solution

HostelMate AI is an AI-powered student accommodation platform that simplifies housing discovery through intelligent recommendations, conversational assistance, and centralized property listings.

Students can search for accommodations based on location, budget, room type, and amenities. The platform analyzes these preferences to recommend the most suitable options, reducing search time and improving decision-making. An AI-powered chat assistant further enhances the experience by providing instant answers about rooms, rent, facilities, and accommodation-related queries. Property owners can also list and manage their properties through a dedicated portal.

## What Makes HostelMate AI Unique

Unlike traditional rental marketplaces, HostelMate AI is built specifically for students and combines multiple services within a single platform:

* AI-powered room recommendations tailored to student preferences.
* Conversational AI assistance for real-time guidance.
* Student-focused filters based on budget, location, occupancy, and amenities.
* Integrated owner listing and property management portal.
* Faster, smarter, and more personalized accommodation discovery.
* Development accelerated using GitHub Copilot for rapid prototyping.

## Key Features

* **AI Room Recommendation Engine** – Personalized accommodation suggestions.
* **Conversational AI Assistant** – Instant support and housing guidance.
* **Property Owner Portal** – Easy room listing and management.
* **Smart Search & Filters** – Efficient room discovery based on preferences.
* **Centralized Housing Platform** – Connects students and accommodation providers in one ecosystem.

## Technology Stack

* Frontend: Streamlit
* Backend: Python
* Data Processing: Pandas
* Data Source: CSV-based property datasets
* Development Tools: Git, GitHub, GitHub Copilot
* AI Components: Recommendation System, Conversational AI

## Impact and Vision

HostelMate AI aims to make student accommodation discovery faster, smarter, and more accessible through Artificial Intelligence. By reducing the complexity of traditional housing searches, it helps students find suitable accommodation with greater confidence and convenience.

Our vision is to build a scalable student housing ecosystem featuring verified listings, advanced AI recommendations, online booking capabilities, and seamless interactions between students and property owners, demonstrating how AI can solve a real-world challenge affecting millions of students.
